# Anwar Ali Sehar - Personal Brand & Digital Solutions Platform

## Overview
This project is a personal brand website for Anwar Ali Sehar, a Local SEO & AI Solutions Specialist. It showcases his portfolio, services, and blog.

## Structure
- **MPA Architecture**: Separate HTML files for major pages (Home, About, Services, Portfolio, Blog, Contact, Admin).
- **Tech Stack**: React 18, TailwindCSS, Trickle Database (Firestore-like).
- **AI Integration**: Multilingual Voice Assistant (English, Urdu, Hindi) powered by AI Agent.
- **Database Tables**: `blog`, `portfolio`, `message`.

## Pages
- `index.html`: Landing page with overview.
- `about.html`: Detailed biography and background.
- `services.html`: Detailed services offered.
- `portfolio.html`: Full portfolio of websites and tools.
- `blog.html`: Articles and insights.
- `contact.html`: Contact form and information.
- `admin.html`: Content management dashboard.
